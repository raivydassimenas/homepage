Mathematics · Programming · Philosophy

Raivydas Šimėnas

I'm an Associcate Professor at the Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics, Vilnius University. My research interests include analytic number theory and machine learning. I also have a strong passion for programming and philosophy, which often intersect with my mathematical work.
